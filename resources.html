Helpful Resources

Find trusted information, support, and guidance on sexuality, relationships, and personal safety

Better Health Channel - Sex Education

Comprehensive guide on discussing sex education with young people, covering various topics and age-appropriate information.

Raising Children Network - Sex Education

Advice for parents on how to approach sex education with their children, including tips for age-appropriate discussions.

Health Direct - Sexual Health

Reliable information on various aspects of sexual health, including STIs, contraception, and reproductive health.

1800RESPECT - Consent

Clear explanation of consent in sexual situations, including what it means and how to recognize it.

eSafety Commissioner - Young People

Resources and advice on staying safe online, including information on cyberbullying, online harassment, and digital footprints.

Headspace

Mental health support services for young people, offering resources, online counseling, and in-person support.

Helplines and Support Services

  • Kids Helpline: 1800 55 1800 - 24/7 phone and online counseling for young people aged 5-25
  • 1800RESPECT: 1800 737 732 - National sexual assault, domestic and family violence counseling service
  • Lifeline: 13 11 14 - Crisis support and suicide prevention services
  • eheadspace: 1800 650 890 - Online and phone support for young people aged 12-25
