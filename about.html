About Our Sexuality Education Program

Empowering Year 10 students with knowledge, understanding, and support

Our Mission

We're here to provide accurate, age-appropriate information that empowers you to make informed decisions and navigate the complexities of sexuality in the modern world. Our goal is to create a safe space for learning, discussion, and growth.

Why It Matters

In today's digital age, you face unique challenges when it comes to sexuality and relationships. We're here to help you:

  • Understand potential risks and consequences of actions online and offline
  • Develop healthy attitudes towards sexuality and relationships
  • Recognize and respect boundaries, both your own and others'
  • Know where to turn for help and support if needed

Our Approach
